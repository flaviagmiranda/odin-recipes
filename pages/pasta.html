Pasta

Descirption

Delicious classic pasta to your sunday dinner with the family.

Prep: 30 mins

Cook: 1 hr

Total: 1 hr 30 mins

Servings: 6

Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Italian sausage links
  • ½ pound lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 (16 ounce) can canned tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can canned tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 bay leaf
    •

    Steps

    1. Step 1

      2. Removed casing from sausage links and cut into 1/2 inch slices. In a large skillet, brown sausage over medium heat for about 10 minutes; remove and set aside.

    2. Step 2

      3. In a large skillet, heat ground beef, olive oil, garlic and onion over medium heat until meat is nicely browned; drain.

    3. Step 3

      4. Pour in tomatoes and tomato sauce; mix in salt, ground black pepper, basil, oregano, bay leaf and cooked sausage. Simmer uncovered for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.

    4. Step 4

      5. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

    5. Step 5

      6. Mix cooked sauce with hot pasta and remove bay leaf from sauce before serving.
